This is the introduction of AutoCompletion with a Close Button.

AutoCompletion with a Close Button is a Module creating by FNST JiangYin Branch based on Angular Material Lib.

When you fill someting in the inputbox as follows, the close button will show.
When you click the close button, what you have inputed will be cleared.
When the inputbox have been cleared, the close button will disppear.

You can have a try with the AutoCompletion inputbox as follows.
